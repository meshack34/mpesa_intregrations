Our Story

LEAD ORGANIZATION-impact training and innovative software solutions.

Founding Background

Lead in Empowerment for accelerated Development(LEAD) is Non-Government Organization founded and Registered on 20 th December December,2022 LEAD Organization was founded on the principles of sustainable technology transfer to address food insecurity, Youth in Agriculture, Environmental concerns, Promoting ICT access to health care services, Provision of Bursary to the needy, Education and social justice

We have strived to create sustainable change through our programs such as Food and nutrition security, Environmental conservation, Enterprise Development, Water harvesting for domestic use and Small irrigation, Youth mentorship program and Youth In Agriculture , Health and Education, Bursary Fund, Pastoral and social justice

Key Milestones

2020: The Beginning

The company was officially founded, with a primary focus on software development and digital skills training in Kenya.

2021: First Major Training Program

LEAD NGO launched its first comprehensive training program, providing over 500 professionals with critical digital skills.

2022: AI-Powered Learning Solutions

We introduced AI-driven learning solutions, helping businesses improve employee performance and engagement.

2023: Expanding Our Reach

LEAD NGO expanded its services to multiple African countries, reaching over 2,000 individuals with transformative digital skills training.

2024: International Partnerships & Growth

We formed strategic partnerships with international organizations, bringing high-quality education to thousands globally.

2025: Launch of Online Academy

We officially launched our online academy, making learning accessible to a global audience with cutting-edge courses.

MAIN OBJECTIVES

To alleviate poverty, promote ICT, access to health care, education and improve both food security and welfare.

  • To empower farmers and citizens by actively participate in decision making and implementation process through appropriates technologies and strategies to revitalize agriculture.
  • To provide a holistic transformation of young people and disadvantaged communities by offering trainings on income generating activities and exposure to life-changing innovations.
  • To ensure that children are safe, healthy and protected at home, in schools and communities.
  • To facilitate the rehabilitation of traumatized children through sports, music, dance, and drama.
  • To support community-led initiatives with focus on using sustainable technologies to enhance development.
  • To build capacity and sensitize children and communities on sexual reproductive health rights and other preventable viral illnesses like HIV and chronic hepatitis.
  • To provide bursaries and learning materials to the less privileged in the community.
